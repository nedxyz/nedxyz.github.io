Info

Webdesign + Development

2022

One possesses too much, one feels too much, and yet so little of this surfeit holds meaning. Design as rebellion and inventory, as interpretation - as self expression. Connected to all that was and all that is to come. Diagonal cycle. For me project 1 is not about outdoing someone on a creative level. I see it as an homage to creativity itself. Taking inspiration from what has already been there - to reinterpret and manipulate it in my own way and by creating the opportunity to let the wearer do the same.
It’s about construction and deconstruction. By taking a classical silhouette and starting from its fundamental form, my designs transform into something timeless but innovative. Architectural, clear but also abstract. Born in Germany, based in Paris. Contrast and contrariness. Rebellion against the inner as well as the outer. Isn't that the embodiment of creativity? Formed by history, by art and architecture - by brutalism. Project 1 is all about the second impression - the impression that lasts.

CGI

2022

I have alwas been fascinated by the craft of architecture and interoir design and how, through the usage of different principles, one is able to set the mood of a space.
This is another space I have designed in Blender. It is situated in the middle of a sea and consists of three floors. Each floor has a different function. The first floor functions as the bedroom, the second floor as a living room and the third floor as a community space.
Here you can see another angle from this project.
I am an avid reader and love to look at how people design their reading spaces. That's how I initialy came up with the concept of this series. My goal is to create several spaces in which I would like to spend time and read.
